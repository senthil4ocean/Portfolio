About Me

As a geospatial enthusiast with a decade of expertise in GIS and Remote Sensing, dedicated to transforming complex data into strategic insights. My relentless pursuit of knowledge drives me to push technological boundaries, embrace interdisciplinary learning, and pioneer innovative applications.

Over the years, I have delivered impactful solutions for high-profile initiatives funded by the Government of India, the World Bank, and ISRO. My work spans critical domains such as land suitability assessment, marine spatial planning, coastal zone management, and satellite-derived bathymetry—advancing science with real-world relevance.

Fuelled by a deep passion for innovation, I combine analytical rigour with creative problem-solving to address environmental and societal challenges. My vision is to bridge the gap between cutting-edge research and actionable, field-ready solutions—empowering sustainable development and climate resilience for the future.
